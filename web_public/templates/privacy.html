Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Who we are

CoI Planner is a free, open-source production planning tool for the game Captain of Industry. The service is operated by an individual developer. If you have any questions, contact us at dogmat.igwt@gmail.com.

What data we collect

Account data

If you choose to sign in, we store your email address in our database. It is used solely to identify your account and send one-time login codes. We do not store passwords.

User content

Production complexes you create, their names, tags, and graph data are stored in our database so you can retrieve them later. Public complexes are visible to other users; private complexes are visible only to you.

IP address

Your IP address is temporarily used for rate-limiting (preventing abuse). It is not stored in our database and is not linked to your account.

Session cookie

We use a single, strictly necessary HTTP session cookie to keep you logged in. This cookie contains only an opaque session ID — no personal data. It expires when you close your browser or log out.

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising cookies.
  • We do not share your data with third parties.
  • We do not sell your data.

Data storage and security

All data is stored on servers located in the European Union. By using this service you acknowledge that your data is processed on EU infrastructure.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — view all your complexes from the Browse tab.
  • Deletion — delete your account and all associated data at any time via Settings → Delete Account. This action is immediate and irreversible.
  • Portability — your complex graphs are accessible via the API at /api/complex/<id>/graph.

For any other requests (e.g. export of your email address from our database), contact us at dogmat.igwt@gmail.com.
